## How it works

This is just a simple chain of flip flops that controlls the outer segments in a seven segment display (as on the demo board)

## How to test

Select the project, set clock frequency to 5 Hz or something similar and reset the project.

## External hardware

Only the hardware (reset, clock and seven segment display) on the demo board are needed for this.
